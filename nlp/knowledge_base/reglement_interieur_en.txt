────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
📋 GENERAL RULES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Student ID badge must be worn at all times on campus
2. Respect for faculty staff, professors, and fellow students is mandatory
3. Any form of violence, harassment, or discrimination is strictly prohibited
4. Property damage results in repair costs + disciplinary action
5. Smoking is prohibited inside all buildings

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
📚 ACADEMIC RULES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

ATTENDANCE:
  • Attendance is mandatory (max 25% absences per subject)
  • Roll call at the beginning of each session
  • Unjustified absences count toward the limit

EXAMS:
  • Exam schedule published 3 weeks in advance
  • Students must arrive 15 minutes before exam
  • No entry 30 minutes after exam start
  • No exit during the first 30 minutes
  • Phone use => immediate expulsion from exam

CHEATING:
  • 1st offense: Zero for the module + written warning
  • 2nd offense: Zero for the semester + disciplinary committee
  • Cheat sheets discovered: Zero for the module
  • Impersonation: Immediate expulsion + legal complaint

PLAGIARISM:
  • Maximum tolerated similarity: 20%
  • Checked via Turnitin for all reports and theses
  • 1st offense: Re-submission required
  • 2nd offense: Zero + warning
  • 3rd offense: Expulsion from the program

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
📱 PHONE & ELECTRONIC DEVICES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  • Phone must be on silent in all classrooms
  • Phone use prohibited during classes
  • Exception: Educational use authorized by professor
  • Audio/video recording prohibited without permission
  • Laptops: Allowed for note-taking only

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
⚖️ DISCIPLINARY SCALE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Level 1: Verbal warning (by professor)
Level 2: Written warning (on student file)
Level 3: Temporary suspension (1-30 days)
Level 4: Semester suspension
Level 5: Permanent expulsion

DISCIPLINARY COMMITTEE:
  • Composed of: Dean + 2 professors + 1 staff + 1 student representative
  • Student has right to defense and representation
  • Decision within 15 days
  • Appeal possible within 30 days

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
🏫 COMMON AREA RULES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

LIBRARY:
  • Silence required
  • Food and drinks prohibited
  • Books returned within 15 days
  • Maximum 3 books per student simultaneously

LABS:
  • No food or drinks
  • Workstation must be cleaned after use
  • Software installation prohibited without authorization
  • Report any technical issue immediately

CAFETERIA:
  • Clean up your table after eating
  • Queue discipline required
  • No food taken outside the cafeteria area
