              LEAVE & ABSENCE POLICY
          Private Faculty of Sciences and Technologies
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
📋 STUDENT ABSENCES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

ATTENDANCE RULES:
  • Attendance is mandatory for all classes (CM, TD, TP)
  • Maximum allowed absences: 25% per subject per semester
  • Exceeding the limit results in exclusion from the subject exam

JUSTIFIED ABSENCES:
  • Medical certificate (submitted within 72h)
  • Official summons (court, military service)
  • Death in the family (3 days)
  • Marriage (2 days)
  • All justifications must be submitted to the secretary

UNJUSTIFIED ABSENCES:
  • 3 unjustified absences → Written warning
  • 5 unjustified absences → Meeting with dean
  • Beyond limit → Exam exclusion for the subject

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
📋 STAFF LEAVE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

ANNUAL LEAVE:
  • Administrative staff: 30 days/year
  • Professors: During academic breaks
  • Requests must be submitted 15 days in advance

SPECIAL LEAVE:
  • Maternity: 60 days
  • Paternity: 3 days
  • Marriage: 3 days
  • Bereaved: 3 days
  • Moving: 1 day

SICK LEAVE:
  • Medical certificate required within 48h
  • Up to 30 days per year (paid)
  • Beyond 30 days: Social security coverage

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
📋 ACADEMIC LEAVE (STUDENTS)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  • Maximum duration: 1 academic year
  • Request must be submitted before December 31
  • Required: Written request + supporting documents
  • Re-enrollment guaranteed upon return
  • Contact: secretariat@fpst.tn | Office 100
