────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
🏅 AVAILABLE SCHOLARSHIPS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. EXCELLENCE SCHOLARSHIP
   • Top of class → 100% tuition waiver
   • 2nd and 3rd in class → 50% discount
   • GPA ≥ 15/20 → 25% discount
   • Criteria: Academic results from the previous year
   • Application: Automatic, no file required

2. SOCIAL SCHOLARSHIP
   • Criteria: Family income < 2x minimum wage
   • Discount: 20% to 50% based on situation
   • Required documents: Income statement, family composition certificate
   • Application deadline: October 15

3. SPECIAL AID
   • Emergency situations (health, family, natural disaster)
   • Amount: Case by case, up to full tuition waiver
   • Contact: social-service@fpst.tn

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
💳 PAYMENT & FINANCIAL CONDITIONS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

TUITION FEES:
   • Bachelor's (all majors): 4,500 DT / year
   • Master's: 5,500 DT / year

PAYMENT OPTIONS:
   • Full payment at registration: -10% discount
   • 3 installments: Sept (40%), Jan (30%), Apr (30%)
   • Monthly payments: Available upon request

REFUND POLICY:
   • Before October 31: 70% refund
   • Before December 31: 30% refund
   • After December 31: No refund
   • Procedure: Written request + original receipt to finance office

CONTACT:
