────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
📝 REGISTRATION PROCEDURE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

STEP 1: Pre-registration
  • Online at inscription.fpst.tn
  • Or at Office 101, Building A
  • Required: National ID, diploma copies, 2 photos

STEP 2: File Submission
  • Complete academic file
  • Tuition fee payment receipt (1st installment)

STEP 3: Validation
  • Verification by registration office (48-72h)
  • Notification by email

STEP 4: Student Card
  • Collect at Office 101
  • Includes: Student ID number, WiFi credentials

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
📄 CERTIFICATE REQUESTS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

ENROLLMENT CERTIFICATE:
  • Via chatbot (instant) or at secretary's office (24-48h)
  • Free for enrolled students
  • Available in PDF and Word format

TRANSCRIPT:
  • Official: Secretary's office (48h) — Stamped and signed
  • Unofficial: Online via student portal
  • Required: Student ID

WORK CERTIFICATE (Staff):
  • Request at HR office (Office 201)
  • Processing: 48-72h

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
📋 COMPLAINT & APPEAL PROCEDURES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

COMPLAINT:
  1. Written description of the issue
  2. Submit to Office 110 or via reclamation@fpst.tn
  3. Acknowledgment: 24h
  4. Processing: 5 business days
  5. Official response: 10 days maximum

GRADE APPEAL:
  1. Written request within 72h of results publication
  2. Re-evaluation by two professors
  3. Response within 10 days
  4. Decision is final

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
🎓 INTERNSHIP PROCEDURES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

BEFORE THE INTERNSHIP:
  1. Find a company (Internship Office or personal search)
  2. Get the internship agreement from Office 203
  3. Submit the signed agreement 15 days before start
  4. The faculty validates and returns the signed copy

DURING THE INTERNSHIP:
  • Regular log of activities
  • Company supervisor evaluates monthly
  • Academic supervisor monitors progress

AFTER THE INTERNSHIP:
  • Submit internship report (30 days max)
  • Defense before a committee (L3 and M2)
  • Final grade: Report (40%) + Defense (30%) + Supervisor evaluation (30%)

CONTACT: stages@fpst.tn | Office 203, Building A
